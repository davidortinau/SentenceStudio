## Work Completed

1. **Feasibility:** Confirmed YoutubeExplode v6.5.6 (already in project) handles all requirements
   - `GetByHandleAsync()` resolves channel URLs like `https://youtube.com/@My_easykorean`
   - `GetUploadsAsync()` streams videos newest-first (perfect for dedup + date filtering)
   - Transcript extraction via existing `YouTubeImportService`

2. **Data Model:** Designed two new server-only entities
   - `MonitoredChannel` — user-added channels, polling metadata, dedup tracking
   - `VideoImport` — discovered videos, import status pipeline, error tracking

3. **Architecture:** Two-phase worker pattern
   - `ChannelPollingWorker` — polls every 6h, creates `VideoImport` records
   - `TranscriptIngestionWorker` — processes pending imports, creates `LearningResource` + vocabulary

4. **Proof of Concept:** Provided complete implementation sketch for `ChannelPollingWorker` with dedup and date cutoff logic

5. **Complexity Reduction:** Simplified from OAuth+webhooks to public scraping; eliminated ~80% of original plan surface area
