# Managing vocabulary words

The Vocabulary page lists every word you have added. Open it from the navigation menu.

## Adding a word

Tap the "Add Word" button in the page header. A form opens where you enter the term, its translation, and optional notes. Save to add it to your active list.

## Editing a word

Tap a word in the list to open the word editor. Change the term, translation, or status and save.

## Deleting a word

Open the word editor and use the delete action. Deleted words are removed from future activities.

## Utilities

The secondary actions menu offers:

- Find Duplicates: locates words with the same term.
- Populate Lemmas: fills in missing lemma forms.
- Fix Swapped Languages: corrects entries where the term and translation were entered in the wrong fields.
- Assign Orphaned Words: associates words that are not attached to any learning resource.
- Bulk Edit Status: changes status for many words at once.
