# Word Association activity

Word Association presents clue words and asks you to decide whether each one is related to a target concept.

## How it works

- You tap Related or Not Related for each clue.
- Correct decisions earn points and increase your score.
- The session summary shows Related count, Not Related count, and total Score.

## High scores

The app tracks weekly and all-time high scores for Word Association. When you beat one, a banner appears in the session summary indicating whether the score was a weekly best or a new all-time high.
