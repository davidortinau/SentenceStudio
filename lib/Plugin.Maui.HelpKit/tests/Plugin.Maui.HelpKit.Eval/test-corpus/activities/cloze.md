# Cloze activity

Cloze is a fill-in-the-blank exercise. A sentence is shown with one word missing and you select the word that best fits the blank.

## Starting a Cloze activity

1. Open the Dashboard.
2. Select the Cloze card.
3. SentenceStudio loads a batch of sentences for your current skill profile and begins the session.

## During the session

- Read the sentence and tap the option that fills the blank correctly.
- The app records each answer as correct or incorrect.

## Session summary

When you complete a round, a summary card appears showing:

- Correct count
- Incorrect count
- Accuracy percentage

You can tap "Continue Practice" to load another round, or "Done" to return to the Dashboard.
