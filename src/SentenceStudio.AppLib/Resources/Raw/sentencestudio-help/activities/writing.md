# Writing Activity

The Writing activity gives you a short prompt and asks you to compose a response in the target language. An AI tutor scores your submission and returns structured feedback.

## Workflow

1. Read the prompt shown at the top of the page.
2. Type your response in the editor. You can revise freely before submitting.
3. Submit to receive feedback.

## Feedback structure

The tutor returns:

- A corrected version of your sentence
- A grammar explanation identifying what changed and why
- A score for accuracy, range, and naturalness
- Vocabulary suggestions tied to your active Skill Profile

## Tips

- Write complete sentences. Fragments are evaluated but score lower on naturalness.
- The tutor respects your target skill level — higher levels receive stricter feedback on word choice and style.
- Saved submissions appear in Activity Log so you can track progress over time.
