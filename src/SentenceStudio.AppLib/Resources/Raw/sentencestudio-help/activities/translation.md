# Translation Activity

Translation asks you to convert a sentence from your native language into the target language (or the reverse, depending on the mode).

## Modes

- **Native to target** — translate the displayed native-language sentence into the target language.
- **Target to native** — translate the displayed target-language sentence into your native language.

## How scoring works

Your submission is sent to the AI tutor along with the reference sentence. The tutor returns a reference translation, a list of meaningful differences, and a numeric similarity score. Exact wording is not required — the tutor accepts multiple valid phrasings.

## Tips

- Focus on meaning first, then polish word choice.
- If you get stuck, submit a partial translation. The tutor highlights what to improve rather than hiding the answer.
- Use the "How Do You Say" activity for quick single-phrase lookups instead of full translation rounds.
