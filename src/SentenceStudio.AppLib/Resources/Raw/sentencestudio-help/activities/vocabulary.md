# Vocabulary

The Vocabulary page lists every word the app has recorded for your profile, including terms you added manually and terms automatically extracted from Learning Resources or activity transcripts.

## What you can do

- **Review** — open a word to see its target and native forms, examples, and related words.
- **Edit** — adjust the display term, native translation, notes, or tags.
- **Quiz** — launch Vocab Quiz to drill a filtered subset.
- **Match** — launch Vocab Matching for a pairing drill.

## How words are added

1. During Learning Resource ingestion, the app extracts candidate vocabulary and prompts you to keep or drop each.
2. After conversations and writing activities, new terms detected in transcripts are suggested for your list.
3. You can add terms manually from the vocabulary detail page.

## Spaced repetition

Words have a due date that shifts based on your recent Cloze and Quiz results. Overdue items are prioritized in drills. The dashboard shows the count of words due today.
