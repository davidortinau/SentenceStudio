# Cloze Activity

Cloze practice shows you a target-language sentence with one word removed. Your job is to type the missing word.

## How it works

1. Choose a Learning Resource or let the app pick the one most relevant to your current plan.
2. The app generates sentences using vocabulary from that resource.
3. For each sentence, the correct answer is compared against your input. Minor typos are tolerated; completely different words are marked wrong.

## Scoring

Each correct answer advances the round. Missed words are queued for a second pass so you see them again before the round ends. Your overall accuracy feeds the vocabulary spaced-repetition schedule — words you miss surface sooner.

## Tips

- Use the Korean keyboard for Korean answers. Romanized input is not accepted.
- If you are unsure, skip instead of guessing. Skipped words do not count against accuracy but do count against completion.
- After a round, review the summary screen to see which words to study before trying again.
