# Conversation

The Conversation activity is a live chat with an AI tutor. Scenarios scope the conversation to a realistic setting such as ordering food, checking into a hotel, or meeting a classmate.

## How a session runs

1. Pick a scenario or accept the suggested one.
2. The tutor opens with a line in the target language.
3. You reply by typing or speaking. Speech is transcribed before being sent.
4. The tutor responds and, when needed, offers a correction sidebar with grammar notes.

## Correction behavior

Rather than breaking flow, corrections appear as a separate panel. You can dismiss them or fold their suggestions into your next reply.

## Ending a session

Finishing a scenario saves a transcript in Activity Log. Vocabulary detected during the chat is proposed for your Vocabulary list.

## Tips

- Start with short sentences and build up as the tutor's replies get longer.
- If the tutor drifts off-topic, say "back to the scenario" — it resets focus.
- Multi-turn context is limited by design to keep costs predictable. Very long sessions eventually drop earlier turns.
