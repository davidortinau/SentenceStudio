# Word Association

Word Association is a rapid-fire drill that trains lexical recall. You are shown a target-language word and asked to produce a related word within a short time limit.

## How it works

1. Start a round. The app picks a seed word from your active vocabulary list.
2. Type any word you consider related — synonym, antonym, same category, or common collocation.
3. The AI scorer judges relatedness and returns a short rationale.
4. Correct answers chain; the next seed is drawn from the field of related words.

## Scoring and time

- Each correct association keeps the chain alive.
- A timeout or rejected answer ends the round.
- Your best chain length is recorded per session.

## Tips

- Breadth beats depth. Common associations score consistently.
- Avoid repeating words already in the chain; the scorer flags repeats.
- Use this activity to warm up before more demanding tasks like Writing.
