# Dashboard

The dashboard is the landing page after sign-in. It shows your daily plan, recent activity, and quick links to the most-used activities.

## Daily plan

The daily plan lists activities suggested for today based on your skill profile and recent history. Tap an item to launch that activity with the right resource pre-selected. Completed items are marked done for that day.

## Progress at a glance

Cards on the dashboard summarize:

- Activities completed this week
- Vocabulary words seen and due for review
- Streak and current skill level

## Quick actions

Common shortcuts on the dashboard:

- Start the next recommended activity
- Open the most recent Learning Resource
- Jump to Vocabulary or Settings

If the dashboard looks empty on a fresh install, complete onboarding first — the plan generator needs your skill profile to produce suggestions.
